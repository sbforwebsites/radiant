Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Apex Plumbing Merchants Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use this website or contact us through it.

1. Who We Are

Apex Plumbing Merchants Ltd is a radiator retailer and installer based in East London.

  • Address: 955 Romford Road, London, E12 5JR
  • Email: sales@apexaimsltd.com
  • Phone: +44 7956 923185

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through this website, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • Your property address (if you request installation)
  • Any additional information you choose to include in your message

We do not collect payment information, bank details, or any sensitive personal data through this website. All purchases are made in person at our shop.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Arrange installation appointments if requested
  • Contact you regarding your enquiry

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we will never sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. How Your Enquiry Is Sent

When you submit the enquiry form on this website, your information is sent directly to our email address via your device's mail application. We do not store your enquiry data on any server — it goes straight to our inbox.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your enquiry information only for as long as necessary to fulfil your request. If no purchase or installation takes place, we do not retain your data beyond the enquiry correspondence. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at sales@apexaimsltd.com.

6. Cookies

This website uses a small number of functional cookies to keep the site working properly. Specifically:

  • Cookie preference cookie: remembers whether you have accepted or declined cookies so we don't ask you again
  • Google Fonts: this site loads fonts from Google's servers, which may set a cookie. Google's privacy policy applies to this. We do not control Google's cookies.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party marketing cookies. If you decline cookies, the site will continue to function normally.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to our use of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@apexaimsltd.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend checking back periodically.
